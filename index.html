Rebirdh is a city goose adventure set in Helsinki, where your species has become the target of a new culling law. Glide, honk, bargain, and quest through the districts to shift public opinion before the vote turns against you.

Each district has its own people to win over, rumors to untangle, and small acts of goose diplomacy to perform. Redeem your species by making Helsinki remember that a threatened bird can still be a neighbor.
